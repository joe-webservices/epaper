About The Pookie

A vibrant platform for student expression and growth

Our Mission

The Pookie is a vibrant, student-led monthly e-newspaper from the Department of English, St. Joseph's College (Autonomous), Tiruchirappalli. Rooted in the belief that "Where Every Quirk Has a Voice," this initiative offers a practical platform for students to express, create and grow.

Our Vision

Conceived to nurture writing, editing and proofreading skills, The Pookie empowers learners to become confident communicators and reflective thinkers. Curated entirely by students - with guidance from friendly faculty and support from the visionary management - the newspaper reflects the dynamic spirit of campus life, academic thought and youthful creativity.

Our Impact

Available in a convenient digital format, each monthly edition invites readers to read, respond and resonate with voices that are bold, curious and wonderfully unique.
